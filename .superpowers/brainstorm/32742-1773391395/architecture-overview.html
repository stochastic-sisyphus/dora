The Three Invariants

Lenses
Pluggable, not enumerated. All share an output schema. New sources addable without restructuring the pipeline. The tool surface is open.
One Render
Single unified surface. State, status, graded sources, notes. Everything renders into the same view regardless of which lens produced it.
Writable Layer
Writing IS control. Not annotation, not buttons. You write, the pipeline enforces. Curation operations emerge from writing.
